Know Your Attorney

John D. Andreopoulos

As a former State Prosecutor in the Pinellas / Pasco Office of the State Attorney, I have handled both Misdemeanor and Felony Prosecutions from the investigative stage through Jury Trial. My experience as a Former Prosecutor has enabled me to become familiar with the practices, procedures and strategies of the Prosecution. In turn, this experience affords me the ability to advise you of the strengths and weaknesses in the prosecution’s case against you.

 

The State Attorney’s Office has a Prosecutor investigating your case. You need an experienced criminal defense attorney to advise you of the possible outcomes of your case. In a FREE initial consultation, I will provide you with a straightforward evaluation of your case. I will advise you of the possible penalties you may be facing and answer all of your questions. Also, I will advise you of the possible dismissal or reduction of the charge against you as well as the possibility of suppressing the evidence against you.
