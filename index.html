Mobile Mechanic

Best Solution for Easy Car Care


Mobile Mechanic is an easy to use tool to fix, tweak and service our car. It provides step-by-step guide for DIY car maintenance and service. Troubleshooter to identify car problems based on chosen symptoms. Mobile Mechanic also comes in handy with Trusted Service Centres around Malaysia for various manufacturers. Mobile Mechanic locate nearby service centres with GPS in case of emergency and provide Google Maps navigation. Service Alert feature enable users to set reminder for oil change based on mileage.

Note: This app was hacked during DiGi Challenge for Change 36-Hour Hackathon, 2014. Development & maintenance of this app has been discontinued.

Features:

  • GPS Location & Maps
  • Service Alert
  • DIY Maintenance with Facebook Sharing
  • Troubleshooter

Awards and Recognition:

  • Finalist - DiGi Challenge for Change Hackathon, 2014

Tags: Android, JSON, Facebook SDK, Google Maps API, PHP, UI/UX, Android Studio
